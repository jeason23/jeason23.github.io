About

I have always been interested in design. I initially pursued design through the lens of architecture, in which I received my undergrad degree. Post-grad, my trajectory in design was different. I have worked as an interior designer, architect, and scenic fabrication. All these various fields have opened my eyes to the possibilities in design but exclusively in the built environment and only allocated to a small group of people. My most recent experience in design was working for a home builder on the creative team. I was tasked with how to make something beautiful with limited resources and a low price point. The company's mission was to be the Ikea of home builders, with the idea that good design could be affordable to the masses. I wanted to continue this idea of good design for the masses and build on the experience of design more tactilely.

While pursuing my Master's degree, I felt I was still missing the knowledge on how to build websites and applications; because of that, I decided to work towards receiving a professional certificate in coding through MIT.
